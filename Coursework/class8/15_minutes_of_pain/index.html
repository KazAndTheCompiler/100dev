15 Minutes of Pain

Learning CSS floats the hard way. Briefly.

Why Floats Exist

Floats were originally designed to let text wrap around images. Developers later abused them into full layout systems.

The Pain

Floats are removed from normal document flow. This causes parent containers to collapse unless cleared.

The Lesson

Understanding floats helps you appreciate why Flexbox and Grid were invented later.
